311 Applied Oral and Behavioral Sciences I
10 hours. Behavioral aspects of team building, professionalism, group learning and health maintenance of the craniofacial complex including examination of teeth, periodontal tissue and oral mucosa. Prerequisite(s): Professional standing.

312 Applied Oral and Behavioral Sciences II
9 hours. The condition and diseases of teeth, periodontal tissues and oral mucosa. Prerequisite(s): DAOB 311; and professional standing.

321 Applied Oral and Behavioral Sciences III
11 hours. The conditions and diseases of teeth, periodontal tissues, oral mucosa, pulp, complete edentulism, partial edentulism and exodontia. 11 hours. Prerequisite(s): Professional standing.

322 Applied Oral and Behavioral Sciences IV
11 hours. The conditions and diseases of teeth, periodontal tissues, oral mucosa, pulp, complete edentulism, partial edentulism and exodontia in the craniofacial complex. 11 hours. Prerequisite(s): DAOB 321; and professional standing.
