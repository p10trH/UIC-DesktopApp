240 Rapid Portuguese for Romance Language Speakers I
4 hours. Characteristics of Portuguese grammar from the perspective of Romance Languages Speakers, leading to basic conversational skills in Portuguese. Prerequisite(s): SPAN 202 or ITAL 201 or FR 232; or consent of the instructor.

241 Rapid Portuguese for Romance Language Speakers II
4 hours. Characteristics of Portuguese grammar from the perspective of speakers of a Romance language, leading to intermediate to advanced conversational and reading skills in Portuguese. Prerequisite(s): Grade of C or better in PORT 240; and sophomore standing or above; or consent of the instructor.
