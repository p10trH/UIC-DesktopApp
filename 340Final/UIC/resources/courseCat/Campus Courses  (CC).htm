120 UIC First-Year Dialogue Seminar
1 hours. Students become familiar with multidisciplinary theory and research on diversity, social justice, culture, and identity, through participation in critical dialogues with each other to explore differences. Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory grading only. Some sections of this course meet during weeks 1-8 and other sections meet during weeks 9-16, as described in the Class Schedule. Prerequisite(s): Open only to freshmen.

0 hours. Information to help students be successful in an online environment.
