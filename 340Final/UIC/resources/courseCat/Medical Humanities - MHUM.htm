494 Special Topics in Medical Humanities
1 TO 4 hours. Presents special topics in selected aspects of medical humanities for health professionals. May be repeated with approval. Students may register in more than one section per term. Approval to repeat course granted by the department. Prerequisite(s): Prerequisites may vary by section, depending upon topic.

496 Independent Study
1 TO 4 hours. Students may arrange with any of the faculty to do independent study on some aspect of communication, history, literature, philosophy, or ethics as it relates to health care.
